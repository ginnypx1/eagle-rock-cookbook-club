Our First Party

1/11/16: "Near & Far" by Heidi Swanson.

Look at how much fun we had!

Our first meeting was a huge success! It turns out that "Near & Far" is a fantastic cookbook. Also, we recruited some seriously talented cooks. Everyone ate, drank, chatted, made new friends, and had a blast!

General Consenus was that our favorite dishes were the Chive Dumplings, the Roasted Tomato Salad, and the Ruby Ginger Juice.

  • Adriene cooked the Spring Carrot & Bean and the Red Lentil Hummus.
  • Anna cooked the Pasta with Cauliflower.
  • Colleen brought some Spicy Tuna & Crispy Rice which should be in the next edition of "Near & Far".
  • Debbie cooked the Mashed Yellow Split Peas.
  • Genevieve cooked the Brown Butter Tortellini.
  • Ginny cooked the Vaghareli Makai and the Easy "Wheat" Bread.
  • Under tutelage from the wise Chef Toshi, Janice cooked the Sake Glazed Mushrooms and the Brussels Sprouts with Karashi.
  • Jena cooked the Chive Dumplings.
  • Julie cooked the Carrot & Sake Salad and the Strong Ginger Snaps.
  • Kit cooked the Sabayon.
  • Korelan cooked the Roasted Tomato Salad.
  • Lauren cooked the Sprout Salad.

  • Melissa cooked the Almond Cake and none of us will ever forgive her dog. (RIP, delicious Almond Cake.)

  • Adriene mixed the Ruby Ginger Juice.
  • Ginny mixed the Saffron Gin & Tonic and the Verde Gin & Tonic.
  • Janice mixed the Plum Wine Sparkler.
